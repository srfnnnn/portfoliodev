Sketch of the typography


The motivation for this design appeared from my strong appreciation for flowers as their inherent beauty, diversity and how their colours effortlessly ehance any environment. I've always been enchanted by flowers, not only for their beauty but also for the feelings they inspire - serenity, happiness and creativity. To convey this personal bond, i decide to integrate my name into this artwork, using the letters of my name as teh foundation. Every letter is artistically crafted with various flowers and leaves, transforming something as simple as a name into piece of floral art. This combination allowed me to merge something personal with something i truly love, leading to a design that showcases both my individuality and my passion for the elegance and charm of nature.


Typography 1

It showcases a lively combination of purples, greens and delicate teal highlights. The shades of purple and lavender evoke feelings of serenity, creativity and luxury. The use of shades of green in the leaves creates a strong natural contrast symbolizing freshness and balance. Teal highlights further ehance the design by adding a hint of brightness and modernity. These colours create a lively and harmonious floral arrangement that feels both refreshing and engaging.

Color scheme: Complementary

Typography 2

It features a harmonious palatte of shades of purples. This colour frequently linked to creativity, elegance and imagination. As is acts as primary colour, it imparts a polished and artistic quality to the piece. This colour palatte expresses an air of elegance and charm, making it ideal for themes that aim to blend floral beauty with a modern and refined vibe.

Colour scheme: Monochromatic

Typography 3

It uses a nature-themed colour scheme led by risch reds, earathy greens. The deep reds evoke feelings of passion, elegance and warthm while the green leaves symbolize development, energy and balance. Teal highlights further enhance by adding a hint of brightness and modernity. These colour create a lively and harmonious floral arrangement that feels both refreshing and engaging.

Colour scheme: Analogous
