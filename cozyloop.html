Moodboard


— This moodbaord is to capture the overall vibe and aesthetic direction for my website, The Cozy Loop. The concept I'm aiming for is a minimalist yet girly theme, built around soft pastel tones that create a calm, inviting and visually cohesive atmosphere. Pastel colours naturally draw attention as they feel gentle, unique and aesthetically pleasing.

The elements in the moodboard work together to express a sense of creativity, comfort and craftmanship. The combination of clean layouts, elegant typography and light colours palattes supports the minimalist feel, while the feminine visuals and textures add warmth and personality.

Brand name: The Cozy Loop

Colour Scheme

Analogous (pastel)

— The colour scheme for The Cozy Loop reflects my preference for a minimalist yet creative design style. I chose a soft, muted palette inspired by warm and calming tones to create a cozy and approachable visual experience. These colours work together to evoke a sense of comfort, balance and subtle elegance, allowing the design to feel expressive without being overwhelming.

This palette is applied consistently across the website — from icons and backgrounds to the moodboard and interface elements — to maintain visual harmony. I value simplicity with a touch of uniqueness, which is why I focused on clean layouts, gentle colours and generous white space. This approach helps create a calm, easy-to-navigate interface while still reflecting my personality and design sensibilities as a developing designer.
