Discover, Guess and Learn

Bloom & Grace is a visually engaging flower-themed quiz app designed to test users’ knowledge of flowers in a simple and interactive way. The app presents a series of image-based questions where users select their answers from dropdown menus. Once all questions are completed, users can submit their responses and instantly receive feedback on their performance.

Key Features


Dropdown Answer Selection


This feature allows users to identify flowers by selecting an answer from a multiple-choice dropdown. A flower image is displayed with a question prompt, encouraging visual recognition before answering.


When users tap “Choose an answer”, a dropdown menu appears with selectable options. This keeps the interface simple and uncluttered while providing an intuitive, mobile-friendly interaction.

Instant Results Feedback


After submitting all answers, a message appears showing the number of correct answers




Craftopia is a creative craft inspiration app designed to help users explore, organize and discover DIY craft ideas. The app showcases different craft categories, such as paper crafts and eco-friendly projects, with visual examples and required materials listed for each craft. Users can also add new crafts, making the app interactive and expandable for personal creativity

App Design Overview

Clean and Friendly Layout


The app uses a vertical card-based layout that is easy to scroll and navigate on mobile devices. Each craft is displayed in a clear, structured card with an image, title and materials list.

Color Palette

Pastel tones like pink, blue and brown are used throughout the interface to create a warm, calming and creative atmosphere that suits a DIY and crafting theme.

Clear Visual Hierarchy

  • The app title “Craftopia” is prominently displayed at the top, making branding clear.
  • Category headers such as “Paper & Card” and “Eco & Upcycling” help users quickly identify different types of crafts.
